BIOGRAPHY

Originally from Jamaica, Eyago Vanni found his love for music early on, drawn to its power to tell stories and connect people. After moving to Canada, he began turning that passion into a purpose, using music as a way to process life, tell his story, and connect with people across cultures.

For him, music is how he breathes, stays grounded, and makes sense of the world. Every beat and lyric carries something real—love, loss, growth, and the lessons that made him stronger. Creating brings him clarity and sharing what he creates reminds him and his listeners that no one is alone.
